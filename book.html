My favourite books

Posted on some date in some year

This is a list of some books I really like. I don't really call them my favourite books, but they're definitely high on my list:

  • The thrones of glass series, by Sarah J. Maas
  • Mistborn, by Brandon Sanderson
  • The Folk of the Air series, by Holly Black
  • Six of Crows duology, by Leigh Bardugo
  • Shades of Magic, by V.E. Schwab

I think those were the most important books that I really liked. I also have some Dutch books I love, but I won't put them here, because it's unlikely that you understand Dutch.
